Welcome to St. Peter's Kyasioni Primary School

Peter’s Kyasioni Primary School offers comprehensive and well-structured academic programs, including Junior Secondary education. The school proudly follows the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC), ensuring that learners develop practical skills, critical thinking abilities, creativity, and values that prepare them for future academic and career success.

Peaceful Learning Environment

St. Peter’s Kyasioni Primary School is located far from the busy main road, offering a quiet and serene environment that is perfect for learning. The peaceful surroundings provide our pupils with a safe, distraction-free atmosphere where they can concentrate, grow academically, and develop confidently.

Core Values and Character Development.

St. Peter’s Kyasioni Primary School is built on the strong foundation of honesty and obedience. We believe that integrity shapes character, and discipline builds success. Through these core values, we nurture responsible, respectful, and trustworthy learners who are prepared to lead with confidence and make a positive impact in society.

ABOUT US

ST PETER'S KYASIONI PRIMARY SCHOOL

At St. Peter’s Kyasioni Primary School, we are committed to nurturing young minds and shaping responsible, confident, and compassionate individuals. Located in a supportive and vibrant community, our school provides a safe and inspiring environment where every child is valued, encouraged, and empowered to reach their full potential. We believe that primary education forms the foundation of a child’s future. That is why we focus not only on academic excellence but also on character development, discipline, integrity, and respect. Our dedicated teachers use engaging and learner-centered approaches to ensure that each pupil develops strong literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking skills. At St. Peter’s Kyasioni Primary School, learning goes beyond the classroom. Through sports, music, environmental activities, leadership programs, and community involvement, our pupils discover their talents and build confidence. We strive to cultivate creativity, teamwork, and a lifelong love for learning. Guided by strong moral values and a spirit of unity, we work closely with parents and guardians to create a nurturing partnership that supports every child’s growth. Together, we are building a generation of learners who are prepared to succeed academically and contribute positively to society.

St Peters Kyasioni plays an important role in conserving the environment. The school promotes tree planting activities, proper waste disposal, and cleanliness within the school compound. Through environmental awareness programs, students are encouraged to protect natural resources and maintain a green, healthy surrounding.
