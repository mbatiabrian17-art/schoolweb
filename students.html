Our Students

Nurturing Excellence Through CBC Education

We follow the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) and offer comprehensive Junior Secondary education designed to equip learners with knowledge, skills, values and competencies for lifelong success.

Students actively participate in co-curricular activities including football, volleyball, handball and netball among others, promoting teamwork, discipline and physical fitness.
